03 / Math
Architecture

One canonical plan. One hash. One signed treasury release.

Rumble events

AutoTipAgent and EventTriggerAgent transform watch, milestone, and native tip events into a shared round ledger.

Canonical settlement

RoundManager serializes deterministic JSON, computes SHA-256, signs the plan hash, verifies it, then executes payouts.

Attestation

After execution, Flow signs the IPFS CID too, so both the allocation plan and archived report are separately attested.
